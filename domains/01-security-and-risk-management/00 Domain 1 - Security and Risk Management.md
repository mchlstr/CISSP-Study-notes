## Domain Summary

Domain 1 sets the foundation for everything else. You must understand:

- **Why** security exists (to protect the business and its assets)
- **How** organizations govern security (frameworks, policies, standards)
- **What** risk means and how to manage it (identify, assess, treat)
- **Who** is responsible (roles, ethics, personnel security)
- **Where** legal and regulatory requirements apply
