## Domain Summary

This domain focuses on:

- **Security models** that define how systems enforce access rules
- **Evaluation criteria** for rating system trustworthiness
- **Cryptography** - the math and protocols behind confidentiality and integrity
- **Physical security** - protecting facilities and hardware
- **Design principles** - building security into systems from the ground up
