## Domain Summary

This domain focuses on:

- **Classifying** data based on sensitivity and value
- **Assigning ownership** and defining responsibilities
- **Protecting privacy** of personal information
- **Managing the lifecycle** of data from creation to destruction
- **Handling** data appropriately in all states (at rest, in transit, in use)
