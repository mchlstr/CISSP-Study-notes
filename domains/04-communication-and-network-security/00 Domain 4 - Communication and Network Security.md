## Domain Summary

This domain focuses on:

- **Models** - OSI 7-layer and TCP/IP 4-layer models
- **Protocols** - how data moves across networks (and their vulnerabilities)
- **Devices** - routers, switches, firewalls, and their security roles
- **Attacks** - common network-level attacks and defenses
- **Secure channels** - VPNs, TLS, and encrypted communications
